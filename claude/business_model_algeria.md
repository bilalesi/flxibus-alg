## Revenue Optimization Strategies

### 1. Dynamic Pricing Optimization

**Factors**:
- Time to departure (0-30 days)
- Historical demand patterns
- Seat occupancy rate
- Competitor pricing
- Day of week / seasonality
- Special events (weddings, holidays)

**Pricing Tiers**:
```
Early Bird (30+ days): -20%
Advance (15-29 days): -10%
Standard (7-14 days): Base price
Late (2-6 days): +15%
Same Day: +30%
Peak Hours/Dates: +40%
```

### 2. Yield Management

**Objective**: Maximize revenue per seat

**Tactics**:
- Overbooking strategy (5-8% based on no-show rates)
- Last-minute discounts for empty seats
- Upgrade offers (premium seats)
- Bundle deals (round-trip discounts)
- Group pricing optimization

### 3. Ancillary Revenue Maximization

**Cross-Selling**:
- Hotels at destination (10% commission)
- Local tours and activities
- Travel insurance (30% commission)
- Food delivery to bus
- Airport transfers

**Upselling**:
- Premium seats (+DZD 200)
- Priority boarding (+DZD 100)
- Flexible tickets (+DZD 150)
- Extra legroom (+DZD 150)

### 4. Partnership Revenue

**Co-Marketing**:
- Tourism boards (route subsidies)
- Hotels (package deals)
- Events (official transport)
- Universities (semester passes)

**B2B Contracts**:
- Corporate travel agreements

## Risk Management & Mitigation

### Revenue Risks

**Mitigation**: Clear contracts, performance-based incentives, transparent dashboard

**Risk**: Regulatory commission caps

### Cost Risks

**Risk**: Technology costs exceed projections

**Risk**: Customer acquisition costs increase

**Risk**: Partner minimum guarantees strain cash
